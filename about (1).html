ABOUT OUR WEBSITE
So glad that you are here. We are students of Kristu Jayanti college and we are making a website to serve you completley free of cost.
Our website is a place where you will find solutions to your medical problems. Our main aim is to let you contact to all the best doctors all over India, specially best doctors in your city.

HOW TO USE?
You have to create an account on our page using you phone number and email-id. Then you will find a section with information about all the doctors who are here with us. If you are suffering from any kind of disease or illness, you can fill our patient form with all your details like your name, your problem, age , any allergy or which kind of medicine you prefer(Ayurvedic, Homeopatheic etc)
Then just submit the form and you will find different doctor's suggestions mostly those who are there in your city.
What you have to do is just open the details of the doctor and directly call them or message them through our website, but only during our working hours.
You will not be able to contact any doctor anytime except the working hours.
